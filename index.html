Dangerous of Drugs in the world

In 2015, an estimated 250 million people have used drugs at least once, accounting for about 5% of the world's adult population. What is even more worrying is that about 29.5 million of these drug addicts suffer from drug abuse diseases, accounting for 0.6% of the global adult population. This means that so many people are harmed by drug abuse and may have become addicted to drugs and need treatment.

Who bears the heaviest burden of drug abuse?

Injecting drug users face the most serious health consequences of drug abuse. There are nearly 12 million IDUs in the world, of which one-eighth (1.6 million) are living with HIV and more than half (6.1 million) are suffering from hepatitis C.
